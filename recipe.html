Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Ingredients Instructions
1 1⁄4 cups flour Preheat fry pan (griddle or skillet for Americans).
1 tablespoon sugar Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
1⁄4 teaspoon cinnamon Mix together wet ingredients and beat into dry mixture until smooth.
1 tablespoon baking powder Fold in chocolate chips.
1⁄4 teaspoon salt Pour or spoon batter into fry pan in desired quantity.
2 eggs Flip when top begins to bubble, then cook a minute more
1 cup milk
4 tablespoons melted butter
3⁄4 teaspoon vanilla
1⁄3 cup chocolate chips

Mac N' Cheese

Ingredients Instructions
8 ounces macaroni noodles (cooked as specified by box) Preheat oven to 350.
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese (shredded and divided) In a 1.5 quart baking dish put half of the macaroni in bottom of dish, followed by half the milk and half the butter. Season with salt and pepper. Cover with half the cheese.
1 cup milk (divided) Repeat above direction for a second layer.
3 tablespoons butter (divided) Bake uncovered for 30 min or until cheese is thoroughly melted.

Homemade Pasta

Ingredients Instructions
2 cups general purpose white flour Add all ingredients to a food processor.
2 eggs Process until mixture starts to form a ball. You should knead the dough for at about ten minutes until it bouncers back when pressed down with your finger.
1 tablespoon olive oil You may want to start with a little less water depending on the size of your eggs and moistness of your flour.
4 tablespoons water Wrap ball in plastic wrap and let rest for about an hour.
